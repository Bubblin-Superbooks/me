I had a feeling that if he didn't know, then all the pleasure of my triumph might go. This is what I said to him:

Dear Roger: [Yes, I called him Roger now.]

Read, mark, learn, and inwardly digest the inclosed thrilling, extraordinary, and absorbing indorsement of Your abused and forsaken
Nora.

How had he the heart not to answer that letter of mine, I wondered.
