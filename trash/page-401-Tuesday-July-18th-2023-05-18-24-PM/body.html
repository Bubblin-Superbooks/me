The only thing he did that approached a caress was that, when his man went ahead of him down the stairs, he stopped in the upper hall, lifted my face, and gave me a long, searching look. Then he said: )

"I'm not likely to think about anything but you, darling." Then he went quickly down the stairs, leaving me sobbing up there.
